Job Search Competition

Building Online Portfolios

Many individuals are creating personal websites to showcase their work, but not all of them are using creative designs or compelling content to stand out.

Networking on LinkedIn

LinkedIn is a popular platform for job seekers to connect with recruiters, but not everyone utilizes it effectively by personalizing their profiles or actively engaging with others.

Sending Mass Applications

Some people rely on applying to as many jobs as possible without tailoring their resumes or cover letters to match the job descriptions.

Standing Out with Personal Branding

Only a few job seekers invest in building a strong personal brand with consistent messaging, making them memorable and professional in their job search.
